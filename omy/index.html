What PRISM Does

Six core capabilities that take you from a bare robot to an autonomous policy — each card links to its dedicated screen.

Start Select Your Robot

Pick your robot from the list and lock in the session. Every screen after this adapts to your selection.

Home → Onboard Add a New Robot

Register a new robot platform with a config file and verify cameras, joints, and 3D model in Devices.

New Robot Setup → Collect Record Demonstrations

Teleoperate the robot while PRISM captures camera feeds, joint data, and success/fail labels per episode.

Record → Curate Clean & Inspect Data

Quality-check recordings, delete bad episodes, add masks, merge datasets — all before training begins.

Data Tools → Train Train a Policy

Choose a dataset and policy type, hit start, and monitor loss curves and GPU usage in real time.

Training → Deploy Run on the Real Robot

Load a trained policy onto the robot and watch it move. If anything goes wrong, take over instantly with Steering.

End-to-End Workflow

Five stages take you from raw demonstrations to autonomous operation — with a human safety net at every step.

01 Collect

Teleoperate the robot and record episodes with success/failure labels.

02 Curate

Clean, augment, and annotate your dataset with built-in visualization and segmentation tools.

03 Train

Pair a dataset with a policy architecture, launch training, and monitor checkpoints and loss curves.

04 Deploy

Load a trained policy onto the robot and run autonomous rollouts in real time.

05 Override

Take manual control when needed, correct the robot, then hand back to the policy seamlessly.
